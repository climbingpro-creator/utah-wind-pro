Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Utah Wind Finder is a weather intelligence application for wind sports enthusiasts in Utah. We take your privacy seriously.

Information We Collect

Utah Wind Finder collects minimal data to provide its services:

  • Weather Preferences: Your selected spots, activity modes, and notification preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Learning Data: Wind prediction accuracy and pattern data is stored server-side to improve forecasting for all users. This data is anonymous and not tied to any individual.
  • Device Information: If you enable push notifications, we store a device token to deliver alerts. No other device information is collected.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity
  • We do not collect your location (GPS) data
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

Third-Party Services

The app retrieves weather data from the following public sources:

  • National Weather Service (NWS) API
  • Synoptic Data / MesoWest API
  • Ambient Weather API (personal weather station data)

These services have their own privacy policies. We only use them to fetch publicly available weather observations and forecasts.

Data Storage

User preferences are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. Server-side data (prediction accuracy, wind pattern learning) is stored on secure cloud infrastructure and contains no personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit utahwindfinder.com.
