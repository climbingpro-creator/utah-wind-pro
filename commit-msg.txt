Fix: ParaglidingPredictor showing 1% when FPS reads 14 mph flyable

The learned weights multipliers (hourly x0.66, seasonal x0.86, calibration)
were cascading to crush probability far below what live sensor data confirms.
When FPS reads flyable conditions (5-20 mph, 110-250 deg), that is ground
truth from the actual launch site sensor.

Changes:
- Add ground truth floor: if FPS reads flyable, south probability cannot
  drop below 55%. Same for UTALP on north side.
- Clamp hourly multipliers to 0.6-1.5 range to prevent extreme crushing
- Prefer FPS actual reading for expectedSpeed over KPVU proxy estimate
