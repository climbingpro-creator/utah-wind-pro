Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

LiftForecast (operated at liftforecast.com, formerly Utah Wind Finder) is a weather intelligence platform for wind sports enthusiasts in Utah. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address and an authentication token to manage your subscription and preferences. Account creation is optional.

Phone Number (SMS Alerts)

If you opt in to text message alerts, we collect your phone number so we can send you weather notifications via SMS. Your phone number is stored securely in our database and is used exclusively for delivering the alerts you configure. We will never share, sell, or use your phone number for marketing purposes. You can remove your phone number at any time from the Text Alerts settings panel.

Alert Preferences

Your alert configuration (wind thresholds, glass conditions, quiet hours, selected locations and activities) is stored server-side to evaluate alert conditions and deliver timely notifications.

Push Notification Tokens

If you enable browser push notifications, we store a device push subscription token to deliver alerts. No other device information is collected.

Learning & Prediction Data

Wind prediction accuracy and pattern data is stored to improve forecasting for all users. This data is anonymous and not tied to any individual.

Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to understand page views and app usage. This data is aggregated and does not include personally identifiable information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your GPS or precise location data
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties for marketing

3. How We Use Your Information

  • SMS Alerts: To send weather and session alerts you have opted in to receive via text message
  • Push Notifications: To deliver browser-based weather alerts
  • Account Management: To authenticate you and manage your subscription tier
  • Service Improvement: To improve wind prediction accuracy using anonymized pattern data

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the platform:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery for text message alerts. Twilio receives your phone number and message content only when you have opted in to SMS alerts. See Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Database and authentication infrastructure. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions. We do not store credit card information. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • National Weather Service (NWS): Public weather data
  • Synoptic Data / MesoWest: Weather station observations
  • Ambient Weather: Personal weather station data

These services have their own privacy policies. We use them to fetch weather observations, deliver notifications, and process payments.

5. SMS/Text Message Terms

  • You may opt in to receive automated text message alerts about weather conditions at your selected locations.
  • Message frequency depends on your alert settings and weather conditions — typically 0 to 5 messages per day.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier.
  • You can opt out at any time by disabling alerts in the Text Alerts settings panel, or by removing your phone number.
  • For help, contact us at the email below.

6. Data Storage & Security

Account data, phone numbers, and alert preferences are stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase, hosted on AWS). Local preferences (theme, display settings) are stored in your browser's localStorage. All API communication uses HTTPS encryption.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your phone number and alert preferences at any time from the app settings. If you delete your account, all associated data (email, phone, preferences, push tokens) will be removed from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at tyler@aspenearth.com.
